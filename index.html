Brief History about Morocco

Welcome to Morocco, a land of deep history and vibrant culture. Our story begins with ancient Phoenicians and Romans who left their mark on our beautiful coastlines and cities like Volubilis. As Islam arrived in the 7th century, the Berbers embraced it while preserving their unique heritage. The mighty Almoravid and Almohad dynasties expanded our influence across North Africa and Spain, making cities like Marrakesh and Fes renowned centers of culture and trade. Today, under the Alaouite dynasty, we continue to thrive as a modern kingdom with a rich tradition. From the bustling souks of Fes to the majestic Sahara, Morocco invites you to explore its historical treasures and warm hospitality.
