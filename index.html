Discover Morocco Through a Moroccan's Eyes

Welcome to WanderMorocco, your personal travel diary for authentic insights about Morocco from a local's perspective. Here, you'll discover the hidden gems and cultural treasures that you won’t find in typical travel guides. My name is Bilal, and I'm a German-Moroccan who was born and raised in Germany but is deeply connected to Moroccan culture, with most of my family living in Tangier. I visit Morocco every year, so I'm well-acquainted with the country and its customs, allowing me to offer you a truly authentic experience. This diary goes beyond just sharing Morocco's rich history—it also provides insider tips to help you explore the country like a true local.
